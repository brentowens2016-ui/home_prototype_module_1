2026-02-01: Remote Monitoring (Live Video/Audio) & Security Policy
- Implemented live video/audio feed support for remote monitoring with strict privacy controls:
  - Video/audio streams are only accessible during active remote support sessions.
  - No video or audio recordings are stored on the backend server; all recordings remain on local devices.
  - Remote agents can view live feeds but cannot download or archive them from the server.
- Security Policy:
  - All remote monitoring endpoints require strong authentication and role-based access.
  - Endpoints are protected against unauthorized access and brute-force attacks.
  - All remote support sessions and actions are logged for audit and compliance.
  - No direct device access is exposed to the public internet; all traffic is encrypted and proxied through secure channels.
  - Regular vulnerability scans and endpoint audits are recommended to prevent agent/server compromise.
