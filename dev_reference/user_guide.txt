# User Guide: mappedhome.com

## Getting Started
- Sign up and select your membership tier.
- Log in to access the dashboard and features.

## Dashboard Overview
- Mapping Editor: Create, edit, and annotate rooms and devices.
- Device Control: Manage smart devices, view status, and trigger actions.
- Emergency Services: Edit escalation contacts, trigger panic button, and view emergency logs.
- Health & Wellness: Access reminders, check-ins, and activity tracking.
- Log Access: View system, agent, and event logs.
- 3D Mapping/AR/VR: Visualize your environment in 3D and enter VR mode.

## Accessibility Features
- Visual alerts and captions for hearing-impaired users.
- Text-to-speech and speech-to-text for visually/mobility-impaired users.
- High-contrast and responsive UI for low-vision users.
- Keyboard navigation and screen-reader support for all controls.

## Tutorials & Tooltips
- Hover over any button or feature for a tooltip describing its function.
- Interactive tutorials guide you through mapping, device control, emergency setup, and accessibility options.
- Emergency protocols and escalation are explained step-by-step in the dashboard.

## Backup & Restore
- All mapping and annotation data is stored locally and can be backed up or restored from the dashboard.
- Emergency and event logs are compressed and stored for compliance and review.

## Support
- Submit tickets via the dashboard for help or feature requests.
- Access AI-powered suggestions and troubleshooting.

## Live Video/Audio Remote Monitoring
- You can view live video and audio feeds from your local devices during remote support sessions.
- No video or audio recordings are stored on the backend server; all recordings remain on your local devices.
- Streams are view-only and cannot be downloaded or archived from the server.
- To access live feeds, click the "Live Video/Audio Feed" button in the dashboard.
- Only authorized remote agents can view your live streams, and a privacy notice is always displayed.
- All remote monitoring actions are logged for your security and compliance.
