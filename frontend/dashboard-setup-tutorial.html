Welcome to MappedHome: Step-by-Step Set_up Tutorial

This guide will help you get started with your MappedHome Dashboard and all user-accessible features. Please follow each step in order for the best experience.

  1. Download & Install the Dashboard Agent
    • Go to the Dashboard Download page and download the installer for your operating system.
    • Run the installer and follow on-screen instructions to complete installation.
    • Launch the Dashboard Agent from your applications menu or desktop shortcut.
  2. Login or Create Your Account
    • Open the Dashboard and click Login/Signup.
    • Enter your registered email and password, or create a new account if you are a new user.
    • Follow any two-factor authentication prompts for security.
  3. Initial Setup: Mapping Your Home
    • Navigate to the Mapping Editor in the Dashboard.
    • Select your floor (1, 2, or 3) or add/remove floors as needed.
    • Use the Room Palette to drag and drop rooms onto the map.
    • Use the Wall Drawing tools to draw or erase walls.
    • Upload a floor plan image if available (Advanced/Elite users).
    • Enter your site address and details in the provided fields.
  4. Adding Devices
    • Use the Device Palette to drag and drop devices (sensors, cameras, speakers, etc.) into rooms.
    • Group devices or show live status overlays (Advanced/Elite users).
  5. Feature Access & Upgrades
    • Access Advanced and Elite features such as audit trail, version history, analytics, and custom icons from the Mapping Editor.
    • If a feature is locked, follow the on-screen prompt to upgrade your tier.
  6. Support & Troubleshooting
    • Open a support ticket from the Dashboard if you need help.
    • Access remote support or view logs as needed.
    • For password resets, use the Forgot/Temp Password option and follow the two-factor process.
  7. Backup & Restore
    • Use the backup/restore options in the Dashboard to save or recover your configuration.
  8. Compliance & Security
    • Review the Terms of Service and legal disclaimers during onboarding.
    • Ensure your password meets the security policy and enable two-factor authentication for all users.
  9. Installing & Connecting Third-Party Devices
    • Ensure your third-party device is compatible with MappedHome (see device documentation or support).
    • Connect the device to your local network according to the manufacturer's instructions.
    • In the Dashboard, go to the Device Palette or Device Management section.
    • Click Add Third-Party Device (or similar option).
    • Enter the device details (type, name, IP address, authentication info if required).
    • Click Scan or Connect to let the system discover and link the device.
    • Once connected, the device will appear in your device list and can be placed on the map or managed like built-in devices.
    • If you encounter issues, check the device's network settings, ensure it is powered on, and consult the device manual or MappedHome support.

Need more help? Use the support ticketing system or contact support directly from the Dashboard.
