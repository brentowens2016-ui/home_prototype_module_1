## Data Retention and Privacy Policy

### Data Retention
- User account data is retained for the duration of the account's existence and deleted upon user request.
- Device mapping, automation, and event logs are retained as long as the user account is active, or until deleted by the user or admin.
- Audit logs and system logs are retained for up to 2 years for security and compliance purposes, unless required longer by law.
- Notification logs (email, SMS, push) are retained for 1 year for troubleshooting and compliance.

### Data Deletion
- Users may request deletion of their account and all associated data at any time via the dashboard or API.
- Upon deletion, all user-identifiable data is removed from the system, including notification preferences and device mappings.
- Some logs may be retained in anonymized form for analytics and security.

### Data Export
- Users may export all data associated with their account at any time via the dashboard or API.
- Export includes account info, notification preferences, and other user-specific settings.

### Privacy
- User data is never shared with third parties except as required for service operation or by law.
- All data is encrypted at rest and in transit.
- Access to user data is restricted to authorized personnel only.

### Compliance
- The system is designed to comply with GDPR, CCPA, and other relevant privacy regulations.
- For questions or requests, contact support via the dashboard or at the provided support email.
